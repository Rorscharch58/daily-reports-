China controls 70-90% of rare earth mining and processing. Any G7 counter-framework will be welcomed by Western mining companies (Rio, Glencore, Anglo) but may provoke Chinese trade retaliation. The US "Project Vault" $12bn stockpile initiative is part of this broader push.
US tariff deadline — G7 window A US tariff negotiation deadline is expiring this month. The G7 Evian summit is the focal point for whether the Trump administration extends, modifies, or escalates the framework.
